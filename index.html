About WBS Planner

A minimal, hierarchical visual planner designed for deep thinking and project breakdown.

  • Reorder: Use arrow buttons on nodes to change hierarchy easily.
  • Colors: Categorize tasks by selecting a node and picking a color.
  • Generate: Paste numbered lists (e.g. 1.1) to auto-generate a tree.
